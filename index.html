About the CSE Alumni Association

The CSE Alumni Association of Premier University is a collective of former students dedicated to fostering lifelong engagement between the alumni community and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. Our association acts as a bridge—connecting the past and present, nurturing professional growth, and preserving the shared values that define our academic journey.

We aim to support our members through mentorship opportunities, career guidance, collaborative events, and knowledge-sharing platforms. Whether it's reunions, seminars, networking meets, or student-alumni mentorship programs, we are committed to building a dynamic, inclusive, and supportive community that adds value to both alumni and current students.

As technology and the world continue to evolve, we believe in staying connected—not only through shared memories but through a commitment to learning, growth, and service to the next generation of computing professionals from Premier University.
