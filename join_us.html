Rules for Joining

  • Applicants must have completed all their academic credits to be eligible for membership in the CSE Alumni Association.
  • Membership is open to graduates of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Premier University.
  • Applicants must provide valid proof of graduation during the registration process.
  • Membership fees, if applicable, must be paid in full during the registration process.
  • Applicants must provide accurate and up-to-date contact information during registration.
  • Members are expected to adhere to the code of conduct of the CSE Alumni Association.
  • Membership privileges are non-transferable and apply only to the registered individual.
  • Members must actively participate in association activities and events whenever possible.
  • The association reserves the right to revoke membership in case of any misconduct or violation of rules.
  • For any inquiries or assistance, please contact us via email at cseaapu@gmail.com.
  • Members must notify the association of any changes to their contact information promptly.
  • Alumni must respect the privacy and confidentiality of other members' information.
  • Members are encouraged to contribute to the association's initiatives and projects.
  • Members must not use the association's name or resources for personal or commercial gain without prior approval.
  • Members are expected to maintain a professional and respectful demeanor during association events.
  • The association may request additional documentation for verification purposes if needed.
  • Members must comply with all local laws and regulations while participating in association activities.
  • Members are encouraged to provide feedback and suggestions to improve the association's operations.
  • Members must not engage in any activities that could harm the reputation of the association.
  • Members are responsible for keeping their membership credentials secure and confidential.
  • The association reserves the right to amend the rules and regulations as necessary.
  • Members must inform the association if they wish to terminate their membership.
  • Members are encouraged to mentor and support current students of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering.
  • Members must not engage in discriminatory or offensive behavior within the association.
  • Members are expected to promote the values and mission of the CSE Alumni Association.
  • Members must not share association-related materials without proper authorization.
